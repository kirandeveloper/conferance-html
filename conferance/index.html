Message From the Organisers

On behalf of the Program planning Committee, we take great pleasure in inviting you to participate in the Best of ASCO® 2025 & other events.

The meeting is scheduled from 27th to 29th June 2025. The theme for this year is "NextGen Oncology: Revolutionizing cutting edge therapy with latest innovations"

The Best of ASCO® is an annual educational initiative that condenses highlights from ASCO's Annual Meeting every year. The Scientific Committee will select specific abstracts for presentation, based on its relevance, impact on day-to-day practice and the needs of oncology professionals in India.

We wish to extend a warm welcome to everyone and look forward to your active participation in making this educational event a grand success.
